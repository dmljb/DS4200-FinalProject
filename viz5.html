Takeaway

This temporal visualization shows how Boston 311 service requests around NEU campus evolve month-by-month throughout the year. The time slider allows you to observe seasonal patterns and identify how complaint volumes and types change between Fall, Spring, and Summer semesters. The timeline bar chart provides an overview of monthly complaint volumes, making it easy to spot peaks and valleys in service requests.

Interaction

Drag the time slider to view any month from January to December. Click the "Play Animation" button to automatically cycle through all months and watch patterns emerge. Click any bar in the timeline chart to jump directly to that month. Hover over complaint circles to see detailed information. The statistics panel updates in real-time to show complaint counts, top categories, average response times, and affected neighborhoods for the selected time period.
