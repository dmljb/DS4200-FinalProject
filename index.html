Analysis of 311 Service Requests Around Northeastern University

A Semester-Based Exploration of Campus-Area Activity

Project Overview

This project will aim to examine how 311 service requests in the surrounding neighborhoods around Northeastern University change based on the current semester. Our main goal will be to understand which complaint types fluctuate over time, which of the areas analyzed experience the highest levels of activity, and how seasonal shifts in campus population affect the city service demand.

We used a combination of Python (Altair) as well as D3.js visualizations to analyze trends in time, space, and complaint type.

Tasks

  • Identify seasonal patterns across complaint types
  • Compare request volumes between semesters
  • Visualize geographic distributions around NU campus
  • Investigate response times and neighborhoods
