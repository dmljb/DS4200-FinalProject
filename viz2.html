Visualization 2: 311 Service Requests Over Time

Takeaway

This time series line chart tracks the daily volume of 311 service requests throughout 2024 in the NEU campus area. The visualization reveals clear weekly patterns, with noticeable dips occurring on weekends when request volumes drop. There's also a significant spike in late August/early September, coinciding with the start of the Fall semester when students return to campus. The daily request volume typically ranges between 600-1,000 requests, with occasional peaks exceeding 1,200 requests during busy periods.

Interaction

This is a static image. The x-axis shows months throughout 2024, making it easy to track seasonal trends and semester transitions.
