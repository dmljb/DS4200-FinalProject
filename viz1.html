Visualization 1: Top 10 Most Common 311 Service Request Types

Takeaway

This bar chart shows the top 10 most common types of 311 service requests in the neighborhoods surrounding Northeastern University. Parking Enforcement dominates with over 63,000 requests, significantly more than any other category. Street Cleaning and Improper Storage of Trash follow as the second and third most common complaint types, each with around 20,000 requests. The visualization reveals that parking and sanitation-related issues are the primary concerns for residents in the campus area.

Interaction

This is a static image showing the complaint types ranked from highest to lowest. The bars are color-coded using a blue gradient scale, where darker blue indicates higher request volumes.
