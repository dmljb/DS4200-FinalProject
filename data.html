Dataset Description

Our dataset is from the official Boston 311 Service Requests, which is hosted on data.boston.gov. It contains all of the non-emergency reports that have been filed since 2011, this includes potholes, trash collection issues, parking violations, noise complaints, public safety issues and much more.

After filtering the data to the surrounding neighborhoods of Northeastern University, we created some semester labels and managed to retain a large amount of observations and features which include:

  • Complaint Type (categorical)
  • Location (continuous coordinates)
  • Department
  • Neighborhood
  • Season / Semester
  • Timestamps (year, month, weekday, hour)
  • Response Time (continuous)
